# Nexus Between Energy Subsidies and Fiscal Deficit in Pakistan

This repository contains the STATA code and outputs for my MPhil thesis
examining the relationship between energy subsidies and fiscal deficit in Pakistan
using annual time series data from 1981 to 2023.

## Research Objective
To analyze the dynamic relationship between energy subsidies and fiscal deficit
and identify key macroeconomic drivers influencing subsidy behavior.
